The government has asked providers to be reasonable with parents, but it can’t force them to stop charging you. ## Free school meals and food vouchers are still available There is still [support for pupils eligible for school meals](https://www.gov.uk/government/news/plans-set-out-to-support-pupils-eligible-for-free-school-meals). This affects SATs, GCSEs, AS levels and A levels. ## The Department for Education will support online learning The Department for Education will support parents so they can keep teaching their children at home. We’re working with the BBC and others to provide suitable resources as soon as possible. For parents with children under 5 years old, see [hungrylittleminds.campaign.gov.uk](https://hungrylittleminds.campaign.gov.uk/).
